Handy Annotated Bibliography Writing Tips: Easy Way to High Grades

March 21, 2017 25 Comments Share

A list presenting the sources (books, websites, articles, etc.) used for exploring a particular subject is called a bibliography. Depending on the formatting style, it is sometimes named as “Works Cited” or “References”. A bibliography provides such bibliographic details about each entry as its author, title, publisher, publication date, etc. An annotation summarizes information provided by a specific source. In its turn, an annotated bibliography presents the entry you are referring to when writing your paper and provides its brief description, i.e. summary. The functions an annotated bibliography may serve depend on the type of your assignment.
